About / 关于我

Storyteller & Technologist

叙事者与技术践行者

I believe design extends far beyond aesthetics—it can serve as a bridge that connects people, reshaping perceptions and fostering mutual understanding. My work sits at the intersection of visual art, digital technology, and immersive storytelling, with a particular focus on translating invisible experiences into tangible, empathetic encounters.

我相信设计的意义远不止于美学——它可以成为连接人与人的桥梁，重塑认知，促进相互理解。我的创作位于视觉艺术、数字技术与沉浸式叙事的交汇点，专注于将不可见的体验转化为可感知的、富有共情的互动 encounter。

From interactive installations that visualize hearing impairment to VR games exploring post-apocalyptic resilience, each project begins with a human story. I am drawn to the poetics of technology: how sensors, light, and code can be woven into narratives that make the marginalized visible and the abstract felt.

从可视化听障体验的交互装置，到探索末日韧性的 VR 游戏，每个项目都始于一个关于人的故事。我被技术的诗性所吸引：传感器、光线与代码如何被编织进叙事，让边缘群体被看见，让抽象情感被触摸。
